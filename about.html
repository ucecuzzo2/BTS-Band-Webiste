ABOUT

BTS which is also known as “Bangtan Boys” is a South Korean K-pop band that have ben known for recently popular songs such as “Butter” and “Dynamite”. Their upcoming rise into fame since their debut in 2013 have captivated millions of fans across the world and becoming one of the most well-known K-pop artist. The members in the group are Jung Kook, V, Jimin, SUGA, Jin, RM, and j-hope.

BTS has even reached the United States, becoming the first South Korean band ever to debut an album that is Number 1 on the US Billboard chart. Collaboration with many artists such as Charlie Puth, Juice WRLD, Nicki Minaj, and Snoop Dog have given sparked national interest in the United States.
